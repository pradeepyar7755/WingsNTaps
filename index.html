OUR STORY

Welcome to
Wings N Taps

Welcome to Wings N Taps in Corpus Christi, TX! We're your destination for scratch-made wings, ice-cold craft beer, and wall-to-wall TVs for every game day. Whether you're here for our legendary buffalo heat wings, hand-breaded tenders, or just to grab a cold one with friends — we've got you covered.

Pull up a seat at our bar where we pour more than just your average draft beer. From rare bourbons to local Texas craft brews, our tap wall has something for everyone. Pair that with our hand-smashed burgers, Nashville hot strips, and 12+ signature sauces — and you've got the best spot on the South Side.
